About us

The IASIS Hospital is 56 bedded fully equipped, State of the Art, Premium Multi Speciality Hospital Located in Vasai-Virar Muncipal Corporation, the Northen Suburban of Greater Mumbai. The Population of Vasai-Virar is More then 15 lakh and for major Medical Care People were going to Mumbai. IASIS is the only Tertiary Care Hospital in Vasai-Virar area and as on today can Adress any Major cases such as Cardiac and Trauma.

Service & Facilities

I.C.U-INTENSIVE CARE UNIT

Our ICU is equipped with Expert lntensivists and Dedicated ICU Nursing Staff thus ensuring prompt Medical/Surgical Care in critical situation.

OT-OPERATION THEATRE

IASIS Hospital is equipped with Three Multifunctional Operation Theatres which are designed at par with International Standards.

DIALYSIS

The Hospital is equipped with multiple Dialysis Beds with all support Infrastructure like RO Plant, Dialyser, Washers etc to ensure high quality of Dialysis. It is Monitored by Certified Technicians under the guidance of Nephrologists round the clock.

IPD

All our Accommodation is fully Air Conditioned and follows all Standard Safety, Infection Control and Operational Protocols. The ambience and environment are made patient friendly to ensure comforts to patient and the bystanders.

OPD

In OPD, Clinical Consultation is provided to Patients which includes History Taking, Clinical Examination, Diagnosis and Providing Prescription to Patients besides Advising Laboratory Tests in some cases. OPD has a Waiting Hall with Chairs, TV, Public Utilities like Drinking Water and Toilets. Wheel Chairs, Trolleys and Attendants are there to help Sick Patients.

DIAGNOSTIC

Full fledged Imaging Department with Multi Slice High Speed CT,Digital Angiography System with DSA Applications,High End Color Doppler Machines,Fluoroscopy and Digital X-Ray Services are available in the hospital round the clock IASIS hospital's Lab performs and reports all Biochemistry,Haematology,Immunology,Microbiology and Pathology Tests in-house.

PHYSIOTHERAPY & REHABILITATION

A well Equipped Physiotherapy Department in IASIS Hospital facilitate faster recovery of our patients and enable patients to restore their quality of life.

PHARMACY & AMBULANCE

Reliable Quality Medicines are Available to Patients 24/7. 24 Hours Cardiac & Non-Cardiac Ambulance Service Available.
